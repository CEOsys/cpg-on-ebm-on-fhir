Resource Profile: Net Effect Estimate

Defining URL:https://www.netzwerk-universitaetsmedizin.de/fhir/cpg-on-ebm-on-fhir/StructureDefinition/net-effect-estimate
Version:0.1.0
Name:NetEffectEstimate
Title:Net Effect Estimate
Status:Draft as of 2022-03-04
Definition:

The net effect is the overall expected effect of a recommendation, calculated by an importance-weighted average of the individual outcome effects

Publisher:Netzwerk Universitätsmedizin (NUM),
Source Resource:XML / JSON / Turtle

The official URL for this profile is: 

https://www.netzwerk-universitaetsmedizin.de/fhir/cpg-on-ebm-on-fhir/StructureDefinition/net-effect-estimate

Guidance

This profile describe the metadata of the net effect for a specific Recommendation and references the Outcome Definition that are aggregated to yield the net effect.

For more information on the net effect, see Alper, B. S. et al. Defining certainty of net benefit: a GRADE concept paper. BMJ Open 9, e027445 (2019).
